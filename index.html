About Me

My name is Thanh Vu. I am a full-time mechanical-electrical engineer. However, photography and art have always been my passion ever since I started taking pictures for VSA in 2013 with the Canon T1i.

When I first got my full-time job, I decided that I would love to pursue photography in the long run. Through the support of friends and people who love my photography, I have been able to capture many amazing pictures from amazing landscapes to beautiful moments of graduation, engagement, maternity, and family.
